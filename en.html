Information

We are currently looking for adults who need cataract surgery to participate in our clinical study. If you are interested in participating, please contact us by email or by phone.


What is cataract?

A cataract is the opacification of the eye's crystalline lens, which affects vision. Cataracts are very common in the elderly, since they are related to aging.

When daily activities are affected, cataract surgery could be an effective treatment. Cataract surgery involves removing the cloudy lens and replacing it with an artificial lens.


What is the purpose of the study?

The purpose of the study is to measure the concentration of the study drug in the eye, following its administration. In this study, the medication will be administered by eye drops, before the cataract surgery.


Who can participate?

We are recruiting adults requiring cataract surgery for both eyes with specific requirements called eligibility criteria*. For more information about these criteria and to find out if you (or your relatives) are potentially eligible for the study, please have a look at the following table:


Eligibility Criteria (non-exhaustive list) Yes No Comments
I am at least 18 years old If no, you can not participate
I have been diagnosed with cataract and surgery is required in both your eyes If no, you can not participate
I am pregnant (if applicable) If yes, you can not participate
I have history of LASIK surgery in one eye only If yes, you can not participate
History (within the previous 3 months) of eyes injury If yes, you can not participate

*Eligibility criteria: These criteria define the target population for the study. The inclusion criteria describes the population who qualify to participate, while the exclusion criteria eliminates the population who are not eligible. These guidelines help ensure the patient's safety.

The list of inclusion and exclusion criteria above is not exhaustive. Other criteria are applicable and your eligibility will be determined by the responsible physician.


How can I participate?

Only those who meet the study eligibility criteria can participate.

We recommend that interested patients review the eligibility criteria to determine if they are admissible for the study. If so, they can contact the research center by email or phone to make an appointment with the physician in charge.

Please, note that your taking part in this study is entirely voluntary. You may refuse to take part in the study or you may stop your participation in the study at any time, without a penalty or loss of benefits.


Confidentiality

For confidentiality reasons, the names of the products, the clinic and the pharmaceutical company will not be disclosed to the participant until contact is established between the participant and the research team. Your medical information will be kept confidential by the study doctor and qualified staff and will not be made publicly available unless disclosure is required by law. All subject information is confidential and handled in compliance with regulatory requirements such as PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Document Act).
